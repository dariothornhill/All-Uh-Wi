We believe reading Caribbean comics should be easy. Since you’re reading this, you do too!

We are open to Caribbean nationals at home in the region from The Greater Antilles in the North, to Guyanas in the South and everywhere in between; as well as those of us living over-and-away (USA, Canada, UK, and beyond).

We’re also big believers in collaboration, so we accept works from teams where at least 50 percent of the team is from the Caribbean.
